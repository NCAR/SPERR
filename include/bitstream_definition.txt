1. SPECK_INT
   Header (9 bytes) + SPECK bitstream
     ^-- num_bitplanes (1 byte) + num_useful_bits (8 bytes)

2. Conditioner
   Booleans (1 byte) + mean (8 bytes) + [custom filter]
     ^-- Is this a constant field?
     ^-- Is there a custom filter?
         (Answering yes to either question will result in a longer conditioner bitstream.)

3. SPECK_FLT
   Conditioner Stream + SPECK_INT Stream
